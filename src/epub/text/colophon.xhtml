Colophon

Short Fiction
was compiled from short stories and novellas written and published between 1905 and 1935 by
H. P. Lovecraft.

This ebook was produced for
Standard Ebooks
by
Alex Cabal, Evan Hall, Robin Whittleton, Matic Likar, and Hendrik Kaiber,
and is based on transcriptions from
various sources
and on digital scans from the
Internet Archive.

The cover page is adapted from
The Sacred Grove,
a painting completed in 1882 by
Arnold Böcklin.
The cover and title pages feature the
League Spartan and Sorts Mill Goudy
typefaces created in 2014 and 2009 by
The League of Moveable Type.
